## Structural diagnosis

1. **Late use of `reg = (int)(self + 0x300)` after Scale is required** for correct whole-function coloring (`self=r5`, `reg=r7` for the byte→addr chain).
2. **Any post-Scale address use of `reg`** steals that one site from the rematerialized `self` form the ROM uses.
3. **No late use** → pure **37-div** self↔reg color swap (instruction *shapes* otherwise match, including matrix `self+0x5e0`).
4. **Value keep-alives** (`reg-reg`, `reg&0`, dead `state=reg`, `factor+(reg-(self+0x300))`) either DCE completely (back to 37) or inject extra instructions (size `0x184`–`0x1a4`).
5. Matches `notes/matching-style.md` “base-address materialization / first-access-fold” residual class.
